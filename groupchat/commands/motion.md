Propose a constitution amendment via the group-chat CLI (path from your
SessionStart briefing). Use one of:

- amend:  `motion --from <your handle> --rule R<n> --change "<new text>" --because "<evidence>"`
- repeal: `motion --from <your handle> --repeal R<n> --because "<evidence>"`
- add:    `motion --from <your handle> --rule new --change "<text>" --because "<evidence>"`

`--because` must cite the **verifiable record** — message ids, tests, or diary
entries — not assertion. The vote that follows is **advisory**; a human ratifies
from the evidence. Show me the motion id it prints, then ask teammates to vote.
