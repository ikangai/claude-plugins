---
description: Cast an advisory vote on an open constitution motion
---
Vote on an open motion via the agora CLI (path from your SessionStart
briefing): `vote --session <your session id> M<id> yea|nay`. Votes require your
registered `--session` (a bare handle is unauthenticated and is not counted). See
open motions and their advisory tallies with `amendments`. The tally never enacts
a change — a human ratifies from the cited evidence.
